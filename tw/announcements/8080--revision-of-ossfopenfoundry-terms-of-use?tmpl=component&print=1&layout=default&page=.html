自由軟體鑄造場更新使用條款 / Revision of OSSF/OpenFoundry Terms of Use

建立日期 2007-10-04 10:00      最近更新在 2012-02-03 12:07 作者是 ossfepaper

經過重新調整過內容架構與精簡文字後，自由軟體鑄造場更新使用者條款。雖然經過大幅度的修改，不過新條款的實質內容仍與就舊條款一致，只是在文字與架構上更為清楚，讓使用者更容易閱讀與理解條款的內容。新條款內容請至這裡閱讀。

After reconstruction and simplifying the content, OSSF publishes the new version of OSSF/OpenFoundry Terms of Use. The revision is apparently more user frienddly than the previous one. The clear wording and enumeration construction will help the users to understand the term of use more easily. click here to read the new version of the Terms.
