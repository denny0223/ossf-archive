Bringing the Power of Simplicity to the Private Cloud

OpenNebula is a simple yet powerful and flexible turnkey open-source solution to build Private Clouds and manage Data Center virtualization based on KVM, LXD and VMware. OpenNebula’s maturity builds upon over a decade of software releases and thousands of enterprise deployments, being widely used by industry and research leaders.
