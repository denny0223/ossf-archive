Security

Enabling business and access control processes for geospatial services and spatial data infrastructures

We develop concepts and ready-to-use software, as well as prototype implementations to enable interoperable business and access control processes for geospatial services and spatial data infrastructures.  In addition, we participate in standardization organizations of the geo-domain and leverage the use of standards by providing a platform for knowledge transfer and early adopter’s implementations.
