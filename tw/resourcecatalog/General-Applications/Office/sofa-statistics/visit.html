Statistics Made Easy

SOFA is a user-friendly statistics, analysis, & reporting program. It is free, with an emphasis on ease of use, learn as you go, and beautiful output.

SOFA lets you display results in an attractive format ready to share. And SOFA will help you learn as you go.

SOFA the People's Choice!

SOFA won the 2012 People's Choice Award in the NZ Open Source Awards - thanks to everyone who voted in support. And SOFA was also a finalist for the Best Open Source Project award.
