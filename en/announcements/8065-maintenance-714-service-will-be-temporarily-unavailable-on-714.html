[Maintenance] 7/14 暫停服務公告 / Service will be temporarily unavailable on 7/14

Created at Wednesday, 30 June 2010 16:00      Last Updated on Friday, 23 July 2010 23:44 Written by ossfepaper

OpenFoundry 用戶大家好:

感謝大家對 OpenFoundry 的支持，為了提供更好的功能服務與操作介面， OpenFoundry 將與 Who's Who 及 OSSF 主網站，進行功能及介面的整合作業，7/14 將暫停服務一天以進行整合工作，並將於 7/15 以全新的版面及架構呈現給各位。如對整合作業有任何的疑問，請隨時來信 This e-mail address is being protected from spambots. You need JavaScript enabled to view it 與我們聯絡。

時間: 7/14(三) 早上 8:00 至 7/15(四) 早上 8:00

Dear OpenFoundry users,

Many thanks for your kindly support for OpenFoundry. In order to provide the better service capability and user interface, OpenFoundry, Who's Who and the OSSF main site are going to have a large-scale function combination and interface integration on Wednesday, 14 JUL, 2010.

The service for OpenFoundry will be temporarily offline from 8:00 am on July 14. After the upgradation has been finished, OSSF will soon present the brand new OpenFoundry layout and structure to you on July 15.

We apologize for any inconvenience that may cause by this upgradation task of OpenFoundry.

Time: From 8:00 am on July 14 to 8:00 am on July 15, 2010.
