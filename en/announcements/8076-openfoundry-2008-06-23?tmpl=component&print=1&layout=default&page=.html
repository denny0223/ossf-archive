OpenFoundry升級/釋出計劃

Created at Monday, 23 June 2008 10:00      Last Updated on Friday, 23 July 2010 23:41 Written by ossfepaper

OpenFoundry 將預計於 2008/06/30 升級至新版系統。 新版系統的原始碼除已公佈於版本控制系統之外，並將於每月月底釋出新的版本。

 

OpenFoundry專案首頁：https://rt.openfoundry.org/Foundry/Project/index.html?Queue=1

OpenFoundry Subversion：https://rt.openfoundry.org/Foundry/Project/VCS/?Queue=1

歡迎您的使用與參與!
