GForge has been discontinued

End of forge.ow2.org and forge.objectweb.org

You are seeing this content because you have accessed a discontinued web service, either https://forge.ow2.org or http://forge.objectweb.org

As it was announced to the Project Leaders on 23 May 2018, the web service at https://forge.ow2.org (and consequently http://forge.objectweb.org) has been effectively discontinued on Monday, 2 Jul 2018. In the initial announcement, Project Leaders were provided with a document that explains the situation and what to do about it.

The content of the GForge project(s) you're trying to access has been migrated and archived as read-only to different places for your convenience. In some cases there are also redirections in place to maintain legacy links compatibility for some time.
