I. Overview

Zlib and libpngare two libraries with regard to the PNG (Portable Network Graphics) sourcecode. Jean-loup Gailly wrote the compression part of zilb and Mark Adler was incharge of the decompression part. Libpng were written by Guy Eric Schalnat, AndreasDilger and Glenn Randers-Pehrson. Since in practice, the two softwares mustcoexist in order to function well, they are often combined and co-licensed under the zlib/libpng.

 

II. Current Status

Almost all the PNG softwares are licensed under zlib/libpng.
